26 / 01 / 2025
January 26, 2025 Event
On this day, we organized our first major beach cleanup of the year. We had excellent turnout with all 2050 registered volunteers showing up. The team was divided into 25 groups covering 5 beaches along the coast. Perfect weather conditions allowed us to achieve 100% of our planned cleanup area. We collected 4000kg of plastic waste which was properly sorted and sent for recycling.
Beaches Cleaned 100% Completed
Volunteers Engaged 2050 2050 Present
Plastic Removed 4000 kg Completed
01 / 02 / 2025
February 1, 2025 Event
Our second major cleanup of the year targeted harder-to-reach beaches along the northern coastline. Despite challenging terrain, we managed to clean 80% of the planned areas. While only 3029 of the expected 3640 volunteers were able to attend due to unexpected rain in some areas, the teams still collected 1050kg of plastic waste from the beaches we could access.
Beaches Cleaned 80% Completed
Volunteers Engaged 3640 3029 Present
Plastic Removed 1050 kg Completed
7 / 02 / 2025
February 7, 2025 Event
This was our largest cleanup event to date, targeting the heavily polluted industrial harbor area. The massive turnout of 3600 volunteers (out of 3900 registered) allowed us to clean 100% of the planned area. We removed an impressive 12,350kg of plastic waste, much of which had accumulated over decades in this historically neglected area.
Beaches Cleaned 100% Completed
Volunteers Engaged 3900 3600 Present
Plastic Removed 12,350 kg Completed
21 / 02 / 2025
February 21, 2025 Event (Ongoing)
This is our current ongoing project targeting the extensive South Bay coastline. The cleanup began on February 21 and is scheduled to continue over several weekends. So far we've completed 20% of the planned area with 1985 volunteers on site (out of 2532 registered). This multi-phase approach allows us to thoroughly clean each section before moving to the next.

Volunteers Engaged 2532 1985 Present
